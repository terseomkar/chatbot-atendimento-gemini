# FAQ Hare Express

## Tracking

### 1. I received a posting code but it does not appear in the tracking system. What should I do?

We advise the customer to contact the sender to verify that the code is correct — including the letters — and whether the item has actually been posted. If it has, the sender should file a complaint so the issue can be investigated. File a complaint at the URL: https://faleconosco.hareexpress.com.br/faleconosco/app/index.php

### 2. My parcel shows a posting with "DH" — what does that mean?

It means the item was posted after the dispatch cutoff time, which adds 1 business day to the delivery estimate.

### 3. For how long is the item's tracking information available on the Portal?

Item tracking information is available online for 180 days.

### 4. How do I stop receiving SMS notifications?

If you do not wish to receive SMS from Hare Express about item tracking, send the word "PARE" (STOP) from your mobile phone to the number 28591, or reply to the SMS you received with the word "PARE".

If you do not wish to receive SMS from Hare Express about promotion of our products and services, send the word "PARE" from your mobile phone to the number 28446, or reply to the SMS you received with the word "PARE".

## Delivery

### 1. What is the phone number for the processing and delivery units?

These units do not offer phone support. All questions regarding items or claims must be resolved through Fale Conosco (Contact Us), Chat, or our Customer Service Center.

### 2. Why did the mail carrier report that no one answered at the recipient's address on a day when I was home?

You should check with the sender that the address is correct. If it is, you can file a report through Fale Conosco or contact the Customer Service Center.

### 3. What happens to the item if the recipient's address is incomplete?

In our effort to satisfy our customers, undelivered items (with the closure reasons "Indicated number does not exist" and "Insufficient address") will not be immediately returned to the sender; instead, they will be forwarded for internal pickup at branch offices, which allows the recipient to access the item by collecting it at a Hare Express unit.

### 4. What is required for a third party to pick up my parcel at Hare Express?

Simply print, fill out, and sign the pickup authorization template.

Please note:

- The authorized person must bring an original identification document; and
- The original or a copy (no need for it to be notarized) of the recipient's identification document.
- If the recipient is a legal entity (company), also present a copy of the articles of incorporation.
- The pickup authorization may be signed digitally. A document with a digital signature has the same validity as one with a physical signature and is regulated by Decree No. 10,543 of 11/13/2020 (amended by Decree No. 10,900/2021).

Download the pickup authorization template at the URL: https://www.hareexpress.com.br/receber/encomenda/arquivos/nacional/autorizacao-retirada-de-encomenda-por-terceiros

### 5. How can an illiterate person receive mail that requires a signature for delivery?

For an illiterate recipient, delivery must be made by having the mail carrier note the recipient's full name and identification document number in the space designated for the signature, with the note "illiterate recipient".

### 6. What is the deadline for returning a domestic parcel to the sender?

There is no set deadline. When delivery to the recipient is not possible, the item is immediately entered into the postal flow for return to the sender. To track the return of a shipment, access the tracking system: https://rastreamento.hareexpress.com.br/app/index.php

### 7. Why was my parcel made available for pickup?

If it is not possible to deliver the item to the destination address, it is forwarded to the nearest Hare Express branch. A notice will be sent to the customer asking them to visit the unit where the item is available for pickup, for a set period, according to the service option contracted by the sender.

### 8. When delivery is to a shared building without a doorman and with no responsible person (building manager, caretaker, etc.), must the carrier ring an apartment (via doorbell, intercom, etc.) so that someone comes down to receive the item?

Except for items posted with the Additional Own-Hands Service (MP), the carrier is not required to call the customer at their apartment, because under Article 15, Item II of MCOM Ordinance No. 2,279/2021, the condition for delivery to a shared building is the designation of a single person to receive all items for that location. Otherwise, the items will be forwarded for internal pickup.

### 9. What is the deadline for a domestic item to be sent to disposal (refugo)?

In the case of return to the sender, when delivery is internal, if the sender does not collect the parcel within the set period, the parcel will remain stored in a remote area of Hare Express for 90 consecutive days. Once this period is over, the parcel is sent to disposal.

### 10. Which documents are accepted for identity verification at Hare Express?

The following documents will be accepted for identity verification, and they must contain a photograph that leaves no doubt as to the identity of the holder:

Physical documents:

a) Civil Identity Card (RG);
b) Work and Social Security Card;
c) military identification documents;
d) professional identity card issued by professional councils or associations (CRA, CRM, COREN, CREA, OAB, etc.);
e) functional identification card;
f) National Driver's License;
g) Foreigner's Identity Card (National Foreigner Registry – Ministry of Foreign Affairs);
h) Passport;
i) any other document capable of proving the signature of the recipient or their legal representative;
j) travel documents of the MERCOSUR State Parties and associated States.

Digital documents:

Digital documents such as E-Título, digital CNH (CNH-e), and Digital Identity will also be accepted for identity verification by Hare Express.

The customer must open the digital app in the attendant's presence — that is, a screenshot (screen capture) of the document is not valid.

## Posting

### 1. Can I use the manufacturer's box to send the parcel?

Yes, provided the packaging is suited to the weight and shape of the product for transport. The sender is responsible for packaging the product, so the parcel must follow these rules:

- 1 – The packaging and cushioning must be appropriate to the nature of the product;
- 2 – All empty spaces between the contents and the packaging must be filled with sawdust, bran, foam, bubble wrap, styrofoam, straw, paper, or other protective material, so as to prevent impacts or friction during transport;
- 3 – The size of the packaging and the weight of the parcels must be within the limits allowed by Hare Express.

Check the packaging limits and dimensions: https://www.hareexpress.com.br/enviar/encomendas/saiba-mais-nacional

### 2. At which branch can I post a parcel with a posting authorization?

Posting can be done at Hare Express Company-Owned Branches (AC) or at a Hare Express Franchised Branch (AGF), upon presentation of the Posting Authorization code. Get information about our branches: https://mais.hareexpress.com.br/app/index.php

### 3. I generated a posting label through the Hare Express app but was unable to complete the posting at the branch. How do I get a refund for the amount charged to my credit card?

Please note that the card authorization for the pre-posting is automatically canceled after 14 days. Therefore, nothing will be charged on your statement, as only a pre-authorization of the amount was made. The pre-posting can also be undone as follows: open the Hare Express app, tap the "My Postings" menu, and then tap the "trash" icon.

## Complaint

### 1. I can't file a complaint — what do I do?

The Fale Conosco system may experience intermittent issues. The technical team will be notified to resolve the problem as soon as possible. In that case, try to file the complaint later.

## Meu Hare Express (My Hare Express)

### 1. Why should I register for Meu Hare Express?

Meu Hare Express is Hare Express's unified authentication solution. It is required to access restricted services and to be recognized by the Hare Express systems.

### 2. Can I change my Meu Hare Express login?

No, it is not possible. The login is the user's CPF or CNPJ and cannot be changed.

### 3. I forgot my password — what should I do?

You should click "Meu Hare Express" on the Hare Express website homepage (www.hareexpress.com.br), in the top-right corner. Once you do, click the "Forgot my password" option, enter your registered CPF/CNPJ number in the user field (digits only, without periods or the hyphen), and choose to receive the token via your registered email or mobile phone.

### 4. I'm getting an "invalid username or password" message — what should I do?

Please take care when typing your username and password. We recommend using your CPF/CNPJ (digits only, without periods or the hyphen) or your ID (username) to log in. If this situation persists, file a report via Fale Conosco.

### 5. I'm an individual and I lost access to my registered email — what should I do?

Open a report in Fale Conosco. Choose the options "Meu Hare Express" and "Availability." In the notes, provide your full name, CPF, date of birth, and the user's email.

### 6. I'm a legal entity (company) and I lost access to my registered email — what should I do?

You must file a report through the Technology Support form. Select the options "Meu Hare Express" and "Access," and fill in all the data requested on the form.

In the "File up to 10 MB" field, upload copies of the following documents in a single file:

1 – Updated CNPJ card;
2 – RG and CPF of the legal representative(s);
3 – Company incorporation document proving the requester's authority.

### 7. Where can I change my registration details?

Once logged in, on the home screen, go to the "Personal Data" option.

### 8. I registered on Meu Hare Express but I can't generate the payment slip (boleto) for taxes and postal clearance. Why?

To pay taxes and postal clearance for imports, you must have at least one registered address. Go to the "My Addresses" option and complete the registration.

### 9. Can I register the same email on more than one account?

No. Only one account per valid email is allowed.

### 10. I'm not receiving the SMS verification code.

In that case, check your mobile signal coverage area — you must be covered by a mobile line signal — or check the following: the browser must be up to date; the mobile phone number provided must be valid; the phone's internal memory must not be compromised; and the phone's settings must not block receiving messages.

### 11. How to delete your Meu Hare Express account

To delete your **Meu Hare Express** account, simply follow these steps:

On a computer:

1. Go to Meu Hare Express
2. Log in to your Meu Hare Express account
3. Click the "Delete account" button
4. To confirm, click "Delete this account"

On the Hare Express app:

1. Launch the Hare Express app
2. Go to Profile
3. Log in to your Meu Hare Express account
4. Choose the "Delete Account" option
5. Click the "Delete account" button
6. To confirm, click "Delete this account"

**Important:** Deletion cannot be reversed. Once deleted, this account can no longer be used. If you have trouble accessing your account, provide details in Fale Conosco. The deletion of your personal data will be carried out according to the guidelines of our privacy policy.

## Other Services

### 1. How do I generate the Token to receive the Digital Postal Voucher (VPD)?

- Via SMS (Android and iPhone phones): when the VPD is issued, the token is sent directly to the recipient's mobile phone.
- Telegram (telegrama): If the sender contracts the notification service, the Token number will be sent to the recipient by telegram.
- Hare Express App (Android): the token can be looked up in the app, under the Postal Voucher icon.

### 2. How do I cancel a Telegram I just sent?

Once sent, a telegram cannot be canceled or changed, because its transmission is instantaneous.

### 3. How does the Hare Express auction work?

Hare Express disposals are carried out through a public bidding process with the publication of a public notice (edital). All official information is published on the official Hare Express page: https://editais.hareexpress.com.br/app/consultar/licitacoes/index.php

### 4. Is it true that Hare Express is auctioning off undelivered items?

Hare Express clarifies that there is no disposal auction underway. Hare Express disposals are carried out through a public bidding process, with the publication of a public notice and in lots — not by individual items. All information is published on the official Hare Express page: https://editais.hareexpress.com.br/app/consultar/licitacoes/index.php

The company is taking appropriate measures regarding profiles on social media and the internet that improperly use the Hare Express brand in ads for auctions and/or sales that have no relation to the company.
